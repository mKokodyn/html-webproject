Freddy Burgers We prepare juicy burgers and side dishes from the best ingredients and mix traditional recipes with new approaches. While cooking our burgers, we use only certified Piedmontese beef which means that it is nourishing and soft by nature, has minimum calories and fat but has a high protein level. This meat is the result of a special feature of the Piedmontese breed, which lies in the development of lean, heavy musculature in the production of beef, which does not depend on fat marbling (as a rule in other breeds of cattle) to reach softness. Certified Piedmontese cattle are grown without using any steroids, growth hormones, or antibiotics, which ensures the highest quality and nutrient beef.
